Here you can find a script which walks through game "Deadline".

About the game: https://ifdb.org/viewgame?id=p976o7x5ies9ltdh

Download and play the game:

    DOS version:
        wget 'https://www.abandoscdn.com/public/aw-remote-download.php?dlc=Zy9EZWFkbGluZS56aXA=' -O deadline.zip
        unzip deadline.zip
        frotz Deadline/Data/Deadline.dat

    Inform version:
        wget https://github.com/historicalsource/deadline/raw/master/COMPILED/deadline.z3
        frotz deadline.z3

Play online:

    https://iplayif.com/?story=https://github.com/historicalsource/deadline/raw/master/COMPILED/deadline.z3
